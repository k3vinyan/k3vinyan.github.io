Arrays and Hashes

April 26, 2014

So what are arrays? To simply put, array is a list of items. Your list can consist of strings, Integers, hashes, and more arrays. By definitions, an indexed collection of objects. Arrays are collections of objects. Hashes are dictionaries where a key points to a value (just like a word points to a definition in a dictionary). Arrays maintain their order, whereas hashes do not.
